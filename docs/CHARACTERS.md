# Character Bible

## T (Protagonist)
- Age: 20
- Role: IT student, part-time builder
- Strengths: fast learner, ships under pressure
- Flaws: avoidant under emotional stress, over-relies on optimization
- Arc: from "I’ll handle it later" to intentional presence

## Mika
- Age: 20
- Role: Design student + old friend of T
- Strengths: emotionally perceptive, artistic discipline
- Flaws: tests others when feeling unseen
- Wants: consistency over grand gestures
- Arc: from guarded waiting to direct vulnerability

## Ren
- Age: 21
- Role: senior student, systems thinker, practical mentor-friend
- Strengths: strategic planning, calm under chaos
- Flaws: can instrumentalize feelings into frameworks
- Wants: to help T avoid self-sabotage
- Arc: learns that metrics can’t substitute empathy

## Side Cast (Act 2+)
- **Ploy:** class rep, deadline enforcer, comic pressure valve
- **Aoi:** cafe barista, accidental philosopher
- **Narin:** startup intern, rival/friend energy

## Relationship Axes (Scored)
- `trust_mika` (emotional closeness)
- `discipline` (consistency / follow-through)
- `clarity` (self-honesty in key choices)

## Tone Notes
- Dialogue should feel natural, concise, modern.
- Avoid melodrama; keep emotion grounded.
- Let silence and short lines carry weight.
